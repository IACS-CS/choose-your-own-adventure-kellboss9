Cheat the test

Getting a good grade is of the highest priority so cheating is the choice you go with. When the test ends a wave of confidence and relief washes over you as you know you just aced your test. Life is good and will continue on as it should. But was it right? As you sit down into your history class a feeling of guilt starts to take hold as you know that you just betrayed your teachers trust and your academic integrity is in question. Your teacher breaks your train of thought as they drop a test in front of your face. You realize today is a test day for most classes. However, unlike for math, you did study for this test. You don't need to cheat this time... but you could. You may have studied but your phone can carry you to another perfect grade. You will never have to study again in fact. It's so tempting. Do you give into cheating or do you do your own work?
