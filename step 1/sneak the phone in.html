You snuck the phone in

Amidst the hustle and bustle of the crowd pouring in, you slip into the school and head directly to class. Your first class of the day is math. You sit down and take out your notebook only to look back up and realize that today is test day. Your nerves start to get the better of you as you search your backpack for something to help you. Thats when you remember that you snuck your phone into the class. Cheating is wrong but if you fail your test you will start the school year with a failing grade. You can kiss your freedom goodbye when your parents find out as well. The choice isn't an easy one. Do you use your phone to cheat or do the right thing and do it yourself?
