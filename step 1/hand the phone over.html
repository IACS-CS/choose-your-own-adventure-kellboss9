You handed the phone over

You wait for the crowd in front of the door to die down before giving your phone to the teacher. After handing it over you start to walk into class before you realize something. Today your first class is math and today there is a math test. You start to panic as you have not studied for this test. You get lost in your mind until the principal spots you still standing in the hall. Your panic attack caused you to stay out of the class. In your panicked state you are not sure what to do. You can either walk over to the principal or you can run down the hall.
